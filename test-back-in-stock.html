Test: Back in Stock Feature

Testing the ability to change vehicle status back to "in-stock"

Test Instructions

  1. Look for vehicles with "Sold" or "On Hold" status
  2. Click the "More Options" button (three dots) for those vehicles
  3. You should see a "Back in Stock" option in the dropdown
  4. Click "Back in Stock" to change the status to "In Stock"
  5. Verify the status badge updates and success message appears

Note: The "Back in Stock" option only appears for vehicles that are currently "Sold" or "On Hold".
